# Consumer Behaviour and Shopping Habits – Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end analysis of consumer behaviour and shopping habits using Power BI.  
The goal of the analysis was to transform raw transactional data into clear, business-oriented insights that support decision-making.

The project focuses on:
- understanding customer purchasing behaviour,
- identifying popular products, categories, and sizes,
- analysing the impact of discounts and subscriptions,
- exploring customer reviews and delivery preferences.

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query (data cleaning and transformation)
- DAX (basic measures and KPIs)
- Data visualisation & dashboard design

---

## Dataset
The dataset was sourced from **Kaggle** and represents anonymised retail transaction data, including:
- customer demographics,
- purchase history,
- product details,
- discounts and subscriptions,
- customer review ratings,
- delivery and payment methods.

---

### 4. Special Offers and Discounts
Analysis of promotional impact:
- percentage of purchases with discounts,
- subscription-based purchases,
- promo code usage,
- comparison of sales with and without discounts.

---

### 5. Customer Reviews and Delivery
Customer satisfaction and logistics insights:
- average customer review rating (1–5 scale),
- review ratings by product,
- delivery methods and their popularity (absolute and percentage share).

---

## Key Insights
- Customer review ratings average around **3.75 out of 5**, indicating generally positive satisfaction.
- Free Shipping and Standard Delivery are the most frequently chosen delivery options.
- Discounts and subscriptions have a noticeable impact on purchase behaviour.
- Clothing dominates product categories, with clear preferences for specific sizes and colours.
